Rules & Instructions:

  • Enter your initial cards one by one. Example: type "10", press Enter, then "7", press Enter.
  • To simulate a hit, enter "+X" (like +7) and press Enter. The system adds that to your total.
  • J/Q/K = 10, Ace = either 1 or 11 (you choose the value when you enter it).
  • Press Bust if you went over 21. You will then enter your final hand and the dealer's hand to analyze the decision.
  • Press Win if you finished without busting. It will also prompt for your final hand and the dealer's hand.
  • Each result is recorded immediately after input. Use Redo to reset the current hand.
